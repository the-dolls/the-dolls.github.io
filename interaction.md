# Rock Band World Tour - Interaction Design

## Core Interactive Components

### 1. Tour Date Calendar & Venue Selector
- **Interactive Calendar**: Monthly view with tour dates highlighted in rock-themed styling
- **Venue Cards**: Clickable venue cards showing concert details, ticket availability, and venue photos
- **Ticket Purchase Flow**: Multi-step process with seat selection and checkout simulation
- **Tour Route Visualization**: Interactive map showing the band's journey across cities

### 2. Music Player & Audio Experience
- **Custom Audio Player**: Rock-styled player with playlist functionality
- **Song Samples**: 30-second previews of popular tracks
- **Album Carousel**: Swipeable album covers with track listings
- **Live Concert Audio**: Ambient crowd sounds and concert audio clips

### 3. Band Member Profiles
- **Interactive Member Cards**: Hover effects revealing detailed bios and instrument specialties
- **Social Media Integration**: Links to individual member social profiles
- **Performance Videos**: Embedded video clips of solo performances
- **Equipment Showcase**: Interactive gear gallery with specifications

### 4. Merchandise Store
- **Product Gallery**: Grid layout with hover effects and quick view modals
- **Shopping Cart**: Functional cart with add/remove capabilities
- **Size/Color Selectors**: Interactive product customization
- **Checkout Process**: Multi-step checkout with form validation

## User Journey Flow

### Homepage Experience
1. **Hero Impact**: Full-screen concert imagery with band logo
2. **Tour Announcement**: Scrolling ticker of upcoming dates
3. **Featured Content**: Latest music video and merchandise highlights
4. **Navigation**: Smooth transitions to dedicated sections

### Tour Section
1. **Calendar View**: Interactive monthly calendar
2. **Date Selection**: Click to reveal venue details and ticket options
3. **Venue Information**: Photos, capacity, and location details
4. **Ticket Booking**: Simulated purchase flow with confirmation

### Music Section
1. **Player Interface**: Custom-designed audio controls
2. **Playlist Management**: Add/remove songs and create custom lists
3. **Album Exploration**: Detailed track information and lyrics
4. **Social Sharing**: Share favorite tracks and playlists

### Store Experience
1. **Product Browsing**: Category filtering and search functionality
2. **Product Details**: Image galleries and size/color options
3. **Cart Management**: Quantity adjustments and total calculations
4. **Purchase Flow**: Form completion and order confirmation

## Technical Implementation
- **Smooth Transitions**: Page-to-page transitions using Anime.js
- **Audio Integration**: Custom player with visual waveforms
- **Interactive Maps**: Venue locations with custom markers
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-optimized touch interactions
- **Loading States**: Professional loading animations between sections

## Engagement Features
- **Newsletter Signup**: Email capture with tour updates
- **Social Media Feed**: Embedded Instagram and Twitter posts
- **Fan Gallery**: User-submitted concert photos
- **VIP Experience**: Exclusive content for registered users
